The full scope of "everything"

Six layers that need to evolve together

1. Atmosphere & Texture
Background layers, grain, gold-as-light-source, ocean depth, post-storm aliveness. The Tarkovsky frame.
2. Typography & Texture
Letterpress feel, visible craft in the forms. Maybe a different display font. Warmer body text.
3. Layout & Space
Asymmetry, negative space as design. Elements that drift slightly off-grid. Dhanishtha rhythm in spacing.
4. Motion & Life
Breathing glow, fog emergence, elements that feel like they were already there becoming visible. Pulse.
5. Page Architecture
Home hero, project case studies (Storm/Investigation/Craft/Emergence), about, contact. Layered depth.
6. Copy & Voice
Positioning statement, about page, project framing. Dinner-party-in-Lisbon voice, not LinkedIn.
